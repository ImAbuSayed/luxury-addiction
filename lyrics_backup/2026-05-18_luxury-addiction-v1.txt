[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Chup chaap is mehfil vich takkda haan straight,
Tere ishaareya di karda haan I wait,
Naina di chamak kare mainu paralyze,
Main haar baitha sab kuch in those eyes.

[Hook 2]
Mithiyan gallan di chaashni sweet as a dream,
Main kharda haan kalla under the beam,
Palkaan di chhaan heth magical sight,
Main labhda haan roshni in the dark night.

[Verse 1]
Teri niliyan akhaan da nasha straight to my soul,
Main kharda haan samne losing control,
Kach wargi aada teri delicate touch,
Main karda haan fikar baby maybe too much.
Hanere ch lishkda ae flawless skin,
Main harna haan jaan let the games begin,
Zulfan di khushboo ch quiet escape,
Main labhda haan sukoon in your perfect shape.

[Pre-Chorus]
Ambraan de taareyan di roshni ch fading away,
Main rokda haan apne jazbaat just to make you stay,
Seene vich lukkia dil mera karda ae crime,
Main karda haan bas sachi gall give me a sign.

[Chorus]
Akhaan vich akhaan paake read my mind,
Main labhda haan sachha pyar leaving past behind,
Kaale is hanere vich shining bright,
Main haar janda apna sab hold tight.
Husna di tezz agg vich burnin' deep,
Main jaagda haan poori raat losing sleep,
Tere bina lagda na eh lonely dil,
Main labhda haan teri raah standin' still.

[Verse 2]
Kaaliyan mashina di ghurr deep in the night,
Main rakhda haan apna jigar seeking the light,
Rahaan te baldi ae agg cold winter breeze,
Main karda haan waade pure handle with ease.
Zehar wargi aada teri toxic affair,
Main jarda haan saare dukh baby I swear,
Yaaran di kachchi yaari layi never back down,
Main jittda haan kalla taking the crown.

[Verse 3]
Apne raste badal litte main just for your sake,
Main karda haan khud nu tabah silent heartbreak,
Pehlan si main pathar warga feeling so cold,
Main labhda haan tera narm dil watch it unfold.
Kachchey dhageyan wangu tutt gaiyan sab the ties,
Main parhda haan jhoothe laare in your pretty eyes,
Beparwah is lambi zindagi ch fading so fast,
Main dabhda haan puraniyan yaadan leaving the past.

[Bridge]
Tere husn da charcha har thaan burning so wild,
Main karda haan tareef like a crazy child,
Saeed di awaz vich bold confessions for you,
Main labhda haan ishq my feelings are true,
Bina tere is zindagi ch everything is gray,
Main kharda haan tere kol just promise to stay.

[Final Chorus]
Akhaan vich akhaan paake read my mind,
Main labhda haan sachha pyar leaving past behind,
Kaale is hanere vich shining bright,
Main haar janda apna sab hold tight.
Husna di tezz agg vich burnin' deep,
Main jaagda haan poori raat losing sleep,
Dubbda haan main saagar ch chasing the sun,
Main takkda haan nawa savera two beating as one.
