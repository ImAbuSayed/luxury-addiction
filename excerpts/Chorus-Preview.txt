Akhaan vich akhaan paake read my mind,
Main labhda haan sachha pyar leaving past behind,
Kaale is hanere vich shining bright,
Main haar janda apna sab hold tight.
Husna di tezz agg vich burnin' deep,
Main jaagda haan poori raat losing sleep,
Tere bina lagda na eh lonely dil,
Main labhda haan teri raah standin' still.
