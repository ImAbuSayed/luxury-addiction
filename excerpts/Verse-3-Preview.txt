Apne raste badal litte main just for your sake,
Main karda haan khud nu tabah silent heartbreak,
Pehlan si main pathar warga feeling so cold,
Main labhda haan tera narm dil watch it unfold.
Kachchey dhageyan wangu tutt gaiyan sab the ties,
Main parhda haan jhoothe laare in your pretty eyes,
Beparwah is lambi zindagi ch fading so fast,
Main dabhda haan puraniyan yaadan leaving the past.
